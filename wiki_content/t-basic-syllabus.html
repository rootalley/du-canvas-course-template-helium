Course Description

The study of the scientific principles as they apply to individuals and groups with application to meeting the nutrition requirements throughout the lifespan. (3 hours)

Prerequisite/Corequisite Courses and Skills

Prerequisite courses: None.

Corequisite courses:

  • NURS 460 Legal, Ethical, and Spiritual Aspects of Healthcare
  • NURS 462 Multi-cultural Global Perspectives in Healthcare

Prerequisite skills: Students should be proficient in word processing of academic papers compliant with APA format.
